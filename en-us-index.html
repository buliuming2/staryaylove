Yay Alliance: The Pact Between the Stars and the Alliance

Text-based Galgame

"Yay Alliance: The Pact Between the Stars and the Alliance" is a text-based Galgame built with Python.

Here, you will play as an ordinary teenager who stumbles into the "Yay Alliance," where you meet five girls with distinct personalities —

🎨 Shika: A quiet and gentle painting girl who captures every fading moment with her brush.

🍰 Kmozi: An energetic cooking girl whose smile is as sweet as egg tarts, yet she has nights when she cries alone.

💻 Web: A silent genius programmer, ostracized by the whole class; code is her only way to talk to the world.

⚓ Relan: A sailing girl who longs for the sea, with the horizon in her eyes and a dream to sail to the end of the world.

🍦 Qtvyu: A dazzling ice pop girl brighter than the sun, whose smile is sweeter than strawberry, shining hard to pay for her mother's medical bills.

Every conversation and every choice affects their fates and endings.

Will you be a partner who protects them, or will you inadvertently push them into darkness?

In the classroom, on the rooftop, in the kitchen… those ordinary days hide the deepest bonds.

Supports Ctrl+T to speed up reading, pink text renders a warm atmosphere,

Multi-ending branching design lets you experience a youth story woven with laughter and tears.

Yay Alliance: The Pact Between the Stars and the Alliance —

For whom are you willing to stay?
